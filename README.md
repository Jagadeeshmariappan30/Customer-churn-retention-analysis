# Customer Churn & Retention Analysis

End-to-end customer churn and retention analysis project focused on identifying churn patterns, understanding customer behavior, and providing data-driven retention strategies.

This project analyzes retail e-commerce customer data to detect at-risk customers, measure revenue impact, and improve customer retention using RFM segmentation and behavioral analysis.

---

## 🔄 Project Workflow

Data Cleaning → Customer Behavior Analysis → RFM Segmentation → Business Insights → Retention Strategy Recommendations

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- Python – Data cleaning and preprocessing
- SQL (MySQL) – Data analysis queries
- Power BI – Dashboard visualization and business insights

---

## 📊 Project Objective

- Identify customers likely to churn  
- Understand key behavioral drivers of churn  
- Segment customers based on value and engagement  
- Measure revenue impact of churn  
- Recommend actionable retention strategies  

---

## 📊 Analysis Methodology

### RFM Analysis

- **Recency** → Time since last purchase  
- **Frequency** → Number of purchases  
- **Monetary** → Total spending  

### Customer Segmentation

- Champions  
- Loyal Customers  
- At Risk Customers  
- Churned Customers  

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

- Customers with **low purchase frequency and high inactivity** are most likely to churn  
- Churn is primarily driven by **reduced engagement and infrequent purchases**  
- Customers relying heavily on **discounts do not show strong retention behavior**  
- **High-value customers contribute significantly to revenue loss when they churn** :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}  

---

## 📈 Customer Segment Insights

- **Champions** → Highly engaged, low churn risk  
- **Loyal Customers** → Consistent purchasing behavior  
- **At Risk Customers** → High value but declining activity  
- **Churned Customers** → Low engagement and minimal contribution  

---

## 💡 Business Insights

- **Inactivity is the strongest indicator of churn**  
- There is a **gap in retaining high-value customers**  
- Heavy **discount dependency does not ensure loyalty**  
- Customers with **consistent behavior drive stable revenue** :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}  

---

## 🎯 Strategic Recommendations

- Target high-risk customers with **personalized communication and offers**  
- Implement **loyalty programs** to improve retention  
- Reduce reliance on discounts and focus on **value-driven strategies**  
- Improve engagement for **declining activity customers** :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}  

---

## 🚀 Additional Actions

- Re-engage inactive customers through campaigns and reminders  
- Focus retention efforts on high-value segments to maximize revenue impact :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
